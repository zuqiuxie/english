Keyboard Shortcuts

### Navigation

Shortcut | Action
---|---
`Down`  | Next item
`Up`  | Previous item
`Alt` `Up`  | First item
`Alt` `Down`  | Last item
`Right`  | Open item
`Left`  | Collapse item
`Tab`  | Next scope
`Shift` `Tab`  | Previous scope
`PgUp`  | Scroll up
`PgDn`  | Scroll down
