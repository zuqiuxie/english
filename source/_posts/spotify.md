Keyboard Shortcuts
------------------



### General I

Shortcut | Action
---|---
`Ctrl` `N`  | Create new playlist
`Ctrl` `X`  | Cut
`Ctrl` `C`  | Copy
`Ctrl` `Alt` `C`  | Copy (alternative link)
`Ctrl` `V`  | Paste
`Del`  | Delete
`Ctrl` `A`  | Select all
`Space`  | Play/pause
`Ctrl` `Right`  | Next track
`Ctrl` `Left`  | Previous track
`Ctrl` `Up`  | Volume up
{.shortcuts}



### General II

Shortcut | Action
---|---
`Ctrl` `Down`  | Volume down
`Ctrl` `Shift` `Down`  | Mute
`Ctrl` `Shift` `Up`  | Max volume
`F1`  | Show help
`Ctrl` `F`  | Filter (in Songs and Playlists)
`Ctrl` `L`  | Give focus to search field
`Alt` `Left`  | Go back
`Alt` `Right`  | Go forward
`Enter`  | Play selected row
`Ctrl` `P`  | Preferences
`Ctrl` `Shift` `W`  | Logout active user
`Alt` `F4`  | Quit
{.shortcuts}
